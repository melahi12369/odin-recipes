Samosa Recipe

Impress your friends with this classic Bengali recipe! Your loved ones will be sure to enjoy!

Description of Recipe

This tasty Bengali snack will knock the socks of anyone who eats or makes them! Complex flavors and a wide range of aromatic and exotic ingredients make for a delicious snack, or even main course.

Ingredients

  • 2 large potatoes, peeled
  • 1 cup peas
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 bay leaf, crushed
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger root
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons chopped green chili peppers
  • 1 quart oil for deep frying
  • 1 package phyllo dough

Steps

  1. Boil potatoes and peas, and then mash them together
  2. In large saucepan, heat oil. Brown cumin seeds and bay leaf together. Mix in onions and ground beef. Cook until beef is browned and onions are soft. Mix in garlic and fresh ginger root. Season with black pepper, sale, cumin, coriander, turmeric, chili powder, cinnamon, and cardamom. Stir in mashed potato mixture. Remove from heat and chill in refrigerator for 1 hour.
  3. Heat oil in large saucepan over high heat
  4. Mix cilantro and green chili peppers in the potato and beef mixture. Place approximately 1 tablespoon of the mixture onto each phyllo sheet. Fold sheets into triangles.
  5. In small batches, fry until golden brown. Drain on paper towels and serve warm
