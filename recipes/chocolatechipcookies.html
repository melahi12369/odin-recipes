Chocolate Chip Cookies

A very straightforward recipe for a very straightforward dessert! Enjoy this very simple recipe that has very tasty results.

Description of Recipe

This will be the best chocolate chip cookie recipe you and your loved ones have ever tried! Very simple ingredients and very simple instructions make this recipe easy to make, and even easier to enjoy. Little twists here and there make them more satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Cream butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth
  3. Beat in eggs one at a time into the sugar mixture
  4. Dissolve baking soda in hot water and add to batter along with salt
  5. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts
  6. Drop cookie batter by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans
  7. Bake for 10 minutes until browned.
