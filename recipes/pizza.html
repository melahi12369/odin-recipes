Pizza Recipe

This recipe is easy to make and is very delicious! Use fresh ingredients if possible.

Description of Recipe

This recipe is great for beginner and expert pizza makers alike! Whether or not you are just starting out making pizzas, we guarantee that this recipe will impress you, your friends, and your family alike! Make sure to follow the recipe to a T the first time. After you feel you've mastered the recipe, free to experiment with the recipe.

Ingredients

Pizza Dough Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups of warm water
  • 1 package of active dry yeast
  • 3 3/4 cups of bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

Pizza Ingredients and Toppings

  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Cornmeal
  • Tomato sauce
  • Grated mozzarella cheese
  • Grated fontina cheese
  • Grated parmesan cheese
  • Crumbled feta cheese
  • Mushrooms
  • Bell peppers
  • Chopped fresh basil
  • Black olives

Steps

  1. Proof the Yeast
  2. Make and knead the pizza dough
  3. Let the dough rise
  4. Divide the dough into two balls
  5. Flatten the dough and round it
  6. Brush dough with olive oil
  7. Sprinkle baking sheet with corn meal
  8. Place pizza on baking sheet
  9. Spread tomato sauce and sprinkle toppings
  10. Bake pizza for 30 minutes at 425 degrees fahrenheit
